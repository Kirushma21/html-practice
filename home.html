The apple (scientific name: Malus domestica).
one of the most widely consumed and cultivated fruits in the world.
Known for its crisp texture, sweet–tart flavor, and high nutritional value.
It is often called a “miracle fruit.

Apples originated in Central Asia, especially around Kazakhstan.
Today they are grown in many countries including the USA, China, India, Turkey, and Poland.
In India, apples are mainly grown in Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Uttarakhand.

Apples are low-calorie and rich in essential nutrients:
Dietary Fiber – improves digestion
Vitamin C – boosts immunity
Potassium – maintains heart health
Antioxidants (like quercetin) – protect cells from damage
Water Content – keeps the body hydrated
